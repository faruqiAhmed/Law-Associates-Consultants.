OUR SERVICES

Corporate Documentations

The Chamber is an expert in corporate documentation. It deals with corporate legal documentations of different corporate firms with total care and dexterity. The documentation includes vetting of documents relating to property, and giving legal opinion thereon, verification of authenticity of documents relating to the property through concerned office, preparing different deeds and agreements, such as Agreement for Sale, Deed of Sale, Deed of Gift, Deed of Partnership, Amicable Partition Deed, Deed of Exchange, Rectification Deed, Lease Deed, Deed of Will, Deed of Redemption, Deed of Revocation, Mortgage Deed, Loan Agreement, Deed of Power of Attorney, Revocation of Power, Letter of Hypothecation, Notarized Power of Attorney, Affidavits, Letter of Undertaking, Further Charge, Letter of Assignment, Letter of Continuity, Letter of Indemnity, Higher Purchase Agreement, Parri-Passu Security Sharing Agreement, Tri-partite Agreement, Trust Deed, Tenancy Agreements, etc. Moreover, we file documents in the RJSC, in case of necessity of our Corporate Clients. We also help our clients in the registration of deeds and documents in the Sub-Registrar office.
